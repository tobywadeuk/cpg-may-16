What It Is

A small group meets for two hours, guided by Pierre, to practice a specific kind of dialogue and attention together. The work happens in the relational field — not on a meditation cushion alone, but with other people, in real time. People tend to leave more present, less reactive, and noticeably better at the conversations that actually matter.

◆
This Session's Theme

Each session explores a specific theme. This one is on love — not romantically, but as a relational skill: how we extend care, how we receive it, what gets in the way. It's the deepest exploration of this theme Pierre has run.

◆
What We'll Do

Guided group practices.Pierre walks the group through specific exercises.

Paired work in small breakouts.Practising directly with other attendees in a smaller setting.

Group reflection.Sharing what came up, what shifted, what's worth taking home.

No prior experience needed. First-timers are the norm, not the exception.

About Pierre

Pierre De Sousa is the founder of Social Sage and has spent the last seven years immersed in the study of consciousness, the mind, and emotional decision-making. He has trained across sixteen psychotherapy systems, lived with a Buddhist nun for a year and a half meditating ten hours a day, and conducted a meta-analysis of indigenous shamanic traditions from cultures around the world.
